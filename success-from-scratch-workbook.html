Phases and Outputs

There are three phases to the building blocks.

  1. Essentials: includes necessary steps to understand your product and customers well enough to get started with Customer Success. Key outputs are Building Bedrock and Standard Success Outcomes.
  2. Efficiency: guides you through building a framework for your customer success program. The key outputs are an Onboarding Outline and automation activities.
  3. Enterprise: focuses on managing a large volume of customers at scale. Outputs include Customer Health Scoring and a Growth Plan.
